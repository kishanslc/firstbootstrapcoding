Rahul

Rahul Sipligunj is a Telugu singer, songwriter, and musician. He shot to fame due to his Hydrabadi folk song. In 2019, he participated and won the popular reality TV show Bigg Boss Telugu (Season 3), which is hosted by Akkineni Nagarjuna. Rahul was interested in singing since his childhood and used to practice on his own

Sreemukhi

Sreemukhi is an Indian actress and television presenter who works in Telugu films. Sreemukhi started her career as a supporting actor in the 2012 film Julai. She made her debut as a lead actress in Prema Ishq

Varun sandhesh

Jeedigunta Varun Sandesh, also known and credited as Varun Sandesh, is an Indian American film actor and television personality known primarily for his works in Telugu cinema. He made his film debut with a leading role in Sekhar Kammula's coming-of-age drama Happy Days in 2007, which was a blockbuster at the box office
